Saturday night Life

Saturday Night Live has been on the air since 1975. In those 39 seasons, there have been 137 castmembers. Of those lucky few, 14 have been black, 1 has been Middle Eastern (Nasim Pedrad), 1 has been Hispanic (Horatio Sanz), and 3 have been mixed race (Rob Schneider, Maya Rudolph, and Fred Armisen). Of those 14 African-Americans (and Maya), only four have been women (Yvonne Hud son, Danitra Vance, Ellen Cleghorne, and Maya Rudolph). Of those four, only one has gained any level of fame and prominence within and beyond the show (Ms. Rudolph).
